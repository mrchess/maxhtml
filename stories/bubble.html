Bubble thread

<mh-thread> + <mh-bubble> is the messaging idiom — bubbles aligned by sender: incoming on the left (surface fill), the current user's own on the right (accent). Mark your own with the boolean attribute me — a self-describing role, exactly like type=button on a secondary button, never a class/data/style/position hook. (The other conversation idiom — avatar + author + body rows for forums/Slack — is Message.)

Example

Anatomy

Side comes from one attribute: a plain <mh-bubble> is incoming (left); <mh-bubble me> is outgoing (right, accent). A <small> inside a bubble is its timestamp; a <header> in the thread is a centred day divider. The conversation list beside it is just a reused mh-list (active row = aria-current), and the composer a reused <form>; the whole two-pane app is mh-layout — see the Messages recipe.

Usage

<mh-thread>
  <header>Today</header>
  <mh-bubble>Did you see the tokens PR?<small>9:01</small></mh-bubble>
  <mh-bubble me>Yeah — just approved it ✅<small>9:03</small></mh-bubble>
</mh-thread>
