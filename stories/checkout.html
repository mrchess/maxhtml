Checkout (recipe)

A multi-step checkout: mh-steps (Cart done · Shipping current · Payment · Review), an mh-alert, a <form> whose fields use mh-grid for the dense address block, a delivery-method radio group, and an order-summary property-panel <table>. Done/current/upcoming all come from one aria-current="step" marker.
