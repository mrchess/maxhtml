Settings (recipe)

A whole settings page from reused composites + the new pieces: mh-app + mh-page shell, mh-tabs section nav, mh-alert notice, a profile <form> (with <select> + field hints), a notifications mh-list of switch rows, an inline radio group, and a <details> danger zone. The model emits markup and zero styling.
