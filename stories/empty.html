Empty state

<mh-empty> is the "nothing here yet" placeholder — an empty list, inbox, or search, or a healthy board with no incidents. A centered column: an optional big leading <strong> glyph, an <h2> headline, a <p>, and an optional <footer> of actions.

Example

Usage

<mh-empty>
  <strong>📭</strong>
  <h2>No notifications</h2>
  <p>You're all caught up.</p>
  <footer><button type=button>Refresh</button></footer>
</mh-empty>
