Live ops / monitoring (recipe)

A monitoring dashboard — chrome is pure reuse (mh-app/mh-page/mh-grid/mh-stat status tiles, mh-card, <table> + mh-badge service status, mh-bars chart). The new ground is the async/transient cluster: a fixed mh-toasts stack (bottom-right), mh-skeleton placeholders + mh-spinner for loading panels, and an mh-empty zero-state. The toasts render with zero styling and zero JS.
