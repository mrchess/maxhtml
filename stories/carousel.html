Carousel

<mh-carousel> is a single-row, snap-paged, horizontally scrolling strip — a product image gallery, a featured-products row, testimonials. Children are the slides: an <img> (full-bleed, pages one at a time), an <mh-card>, or a <figure>. Scroll-snap gives the paging feel with zero JS. Distinct from mh-gallery (a wrapping tile grid) and mh-board (fixed card lanes).

Usage

<mh-carousel>
  <img src="…"><img src="…"><img src="…">       <!-- image gallery -->
</mh-carousel>

<mh-carousel>
  <mh-card>…</mh-card><mh-card>…</mh-card>      <!-- featured row -->
</mh-carousel>
