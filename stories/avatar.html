Avatar

A round container for a person. Holds, in order of preference: an <img> (photo), initials text when the name is known, or <mh-icon name="user"> for an anonymous/unknown person. No emoji faces — the fallbacks are real, themeable, and recolorable.

Examples — image, initials, anonymous

Usage

<mh-avatar><img src="..." alt=""></mh-avatar>       <!-- photo -->
<mh-avatar>AL</mh-avatar>                          <!-- initials (name known) -->
<mh-avatar><mh-icon name="user"></mh-icon></mh-avatar>  <!-- anonymous -->
