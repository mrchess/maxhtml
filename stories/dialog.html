Dialog (behavior layer)

A native <dialog> — modal, focus-trapped, Esc-to-close, all for free from the platform. Open it from any <button commandfor="id">; it closes on any dialog button, a backdrop click, or Esc. Wiring is one global listener in mh-dialog.js.

Example — try it

Remove friend?

Ada Lovelace will be removed from your friends. You can add them again later.

Slots

A <header> (title), body content or a <form>, and a <footer> of actions. Primary vs secondary is <button> vs <button type=button> — both close; wire the primary to do the action.

Usage

<button commandfor="remove">Remove friend…</button>

<dialog id="remove">
  <header>Remove friend?</header>
  <p>Ada Lovelace will be removed from your friends.</p>
  <footer>
    <button type=button>Cancel</button>
    <button>Remove</button>
  </footer>
</dialog>

<script src="mh-dialog.js"></script>
