Stat tile + Grid

<mh-grid> is a responsive auto-fit grid; <mh-stat> is a metric tile with slots <header> (label), <strong> (value), <small> (delta). Drop tiles into the grid and they wrap on their own.

Example

Usage

<mh-grid>
  <mh-stat><header>Revenue</header><strong>$48.2k</strong><small>+12%</small></mh-stat>
  ...
</mh-grid>
