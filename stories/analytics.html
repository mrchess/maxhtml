Analytics (recipe)

A dashboard with real charts — mh-app + mh-page + a date-range mh-slots + a mh-grid of mh-stat tiles, then line, bar, and donut charts (each framed by a reused mh-card) and a <table>. The chrome is fully on-rails; the chart marks ride the data-hook / SVG boundary (see Charts).
