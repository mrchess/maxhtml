Split (composite)

An asymmetric two-column content split: a narrow rail (stats, a TOC, an aside) beside a wider main column, at a fixed ~1:2 ratio. The two children map to the two columns by source order — rail first, main second — and they stack on a narrow viewport. This is what mh-grid (symmetric, equal auto-fit tracks) and mh-app/mh-layout (full-height app shells with a fixed-px sidebar) can't be.

Usage

Children are usually region-arrangers themselves — an mh-grid of stats beside an mh-grid of cards, or a block of prose beside an aside.

<mh-split>
  <mh-grid>...stats rail...</mh-grid>   <!-- narrow, first -->
  <mh-card>...main...</mh-card>          <!-- wider, second -->
</mh-split>
