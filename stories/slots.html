Slots

<mh-slots> is a dense, equal grid of selectable chips — booking time slots, and just as well filters, sizes, or seats. Children are <button type=button> chips: mark the chosen one with aria-pressed="true" and unavailable ones with disabled — both native and edit-proof. Unlike mh-grid (wide 180px tracks) the cells are tight and uniform, sized to short labels.

Example

Anatomy

State rides on native attributes only: aria-pressed="true" = the chosen slot (filled), disabled = taken (dimmed, not clickable). No class, no position counting — reorder or edit freely.

Usage

<mh-slots>
  <button type=button>9:00</button>
  <button type=button disabled>10:00</button>        <!-- taken -->
  <button type=button aria-pressed="true">11:00</button> <!-- chosen -->
</mh-slots>
