Accordion (native <details>)

No new tag — the kit styles native <details>/<summary>, like it styles <dialog> and <table>. Native open/close, zero JS. The <summary> is the clickable header (with a +/− affordance); everything after it is the panel. Stack several for an accordion — FAQ, advanced settings, a danger zone.
