Landing page (recipe)

A marketing page in the split-proof style — navbar, centered hero, a feature grid, a mh-split "proof" band (a stats rail beside a wider highlight), and a dark mh-prompt CTA. Assembled from mh-navbar + mh-hero + mh-section + mh-grid/mh-card + mh-stat + the two new composites mh-split and mh-prompt. Built entirely from MaxHTML: no div, no class, no styling emitted.
