2 column design

A two-column application shell: a fixed-width <mh-sidemenu> on the left and a fluid <main> content area on the right. You are looking at it right now — this Storybook is built with it.

Anatomy

<mh-layout> is a CSS grid with two columns. Drop an <mh-sidemenu> and a <main> inside it as direct children — no wrapper divs, no classes.

Usage

<mh-layout>
  <mh-sidemenu>...</mh-sidemenu>
  <main>...</main>
</mh-layout>
