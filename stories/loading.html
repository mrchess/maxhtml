Loading: skeleton · spinner · progress

Three native-feeling loading atoms. <mh-skeleton> is a pulsing content placeholder (stack a few for a paragraph or a card). <mh-spinner> is an indeterminate ring for in-progress actions. For a determinate bar use native <progress>.

Example

Usage

<mh-skeleton></mh-skeleton>        <!-- placeholder line; stack a few -->
<mh-spinner></mh-spinner> Streaming…  <!-- indeterminate -->
<progress value="62" max="100"></progress>  <!-- determinate -->
