Alert

<mh-alert> is an inline notice — "changes saved", "verify your email", a form-level error. Like <mh-badge>, severity colour can't ride a class (the kit forbids variant hooks), so it rides a leading emoji as content (ℹ️ ✅ ⚠️ 🔴) — the same convention as badges. An optional trailing <button> floats to the right.

Example

Usage

<mh-alert>✅ <strong>Saved.</strong> Your profile was updated.</mh-alert>
<mh-alert>⚠️ <strong>Verify your email.</strong> …<button type=button>Resend</button></mh-alert>

Known limit: the box is neutral; severity is the emoji, not a colour fill — the same colour-variant wall as mh-badge.
