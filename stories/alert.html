Alert

<mh-alert> is an inline notice — "changes saved", "verify your email", a form-level error. Severity rides the shared tone axis (success / warning / danger / info): it tints the left border and prepends a matching severity icon, drawn from the stylesheet with zero emitted markup. No attribute = a neutral accent notice. An optional trailing <button> floats to the right.

Example

Usage

<mh-alert tone="success"><strong>Saved.</strong> Your profile was updated.</mh-alert>
<mh-alert tone="warning"><strong>Verify your email.</strong> …<button type=button>Resend</button></mh-alert>

Severity is real themeable colour now (border + icon, tinted by the --mh-success/-warning/-danger/-info tokens) — the colour-variant wall converted into the shared tone primitive, the same one mh-badge uses.
