Forum (recipe)

A forum home (thread list) — mh-app + mh-page + mh-list of topics (title link + author/meta + a mh-badge reply count), with a <dialog> new-topic form. No new composite at all — the list view is pure mh-list/mh-item reuse; the conversation screen (where mh-message lives) is Thread.
